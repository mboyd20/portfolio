Princeton University

Why do you want to go to Princeton?

Princeton is an ivy league and one of the best schools in the world. It would grant me one of the best educations and challenge my inquisitve mind. It's also one of the top 20 most prestigious law schools in the country. Princeton is also one of the world's foremost research universities with connections to more than 40 Nobel laureates, 17 winners of the National Medal of Science and five recipients of the National Humanities Medal.


Tuition price?

Princeton is a yearly tuition of a little over $50,000 and a grand total of over $200,000 for all four years.

What are the amentities?

Princeton is unique in being a great research university with a profound commitment to the liberal arts. As a student, you benefit from the extraordinary resources of a world-class teaching and research university, and low student-to-faculty ratio means you are able to develop close working relationships with professors who are leaders in their fields.

What are the requirements to apply?

  • 3.9 GPA or higher
  • 1570 SAT
  • 35 ACT

They also look for students with intellectual curiosity, who have pursued and achieved academic excellence and students with strong personal and extracurricular accomplishments.
