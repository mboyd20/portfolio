What is the job of a lawyer?

A lawyer is someone who advises and represents clients in criminal or civil proceedings and in other legal matters. Their job also includes communicating with clients, colleagues, judges, and others involved in a case. Conduct research and analysis of legal issues. Interpret laws, rulings, and regulations for individuals and businesses.


What kind of lawyer do you want to be?
